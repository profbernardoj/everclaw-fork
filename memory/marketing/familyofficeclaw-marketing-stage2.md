## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"The family office that runs on your terms. Not theirs."**

FamilyOfficeClaw is the AI assistant for family offices — advisor coordination, document organization, succession planning, and family governance. All running locally with absolute privacy.

### Value Proposition Statement
> You have accountants, lawyers, investment advisors, estate planners — each with their own systems. Succession planning is a folder no one opens.
> 
> Connect FamilyOfficeClaw and get an AI that:
> - Centralizes advisor contacts and relationships
> - Organizes estate documents, trusts, deeds, insurance
> - Tracks succession milestones and reminders
> - Manages family governance and meeting records
> - Runs locally — your family's most sensitive data stays private
