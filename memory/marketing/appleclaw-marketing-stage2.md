## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"AI that speaks fluent Apple."**

AppleClaw is the AI assistant for Apple users — native integration with Notes, Reminders, Calendar, and iCloud.

### Value Proposition Statement
> You live in the Apple ecosystem. Notes, Reminders, Calendar, iCloud. But most AI tools want their own apps, their own clouds.
> 
> Connect AppleClaw and get an AI that:
> - Reads and organizes your Apple Notes
> - Creates and tracks your Reminders
> - Integrates with Calendar and iCloud
> - Works with Siri Shortcuts
> - Runs locally — your Apple data stays in your ecosystem
