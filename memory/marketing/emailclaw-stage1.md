**Created:** February 19, 2026

## Target Persona

**Primary:** Productivity-focused users, email power users, professionals with inbox overwhelm, GTD enthusiasts

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-55
- Technical: Moderate (comfortable with email clients, productivity tools, automation)
- Values: Efficiency, inbox zero, time management, professional communication
- Pain points: Email overwhelm, important messages buried, time wasted on email, follow-up failures

**Psychographic Profile:**
- "My inbox is my to-do list, and it's broken" mindset
- Receives 50-200+ emails per day
- Has tried multiple email apps and systems
- Values inbox zero but rarely achieves it
- Professional reputation tied to email responsiveness

### The Solution
**EmailClaw: Your intelligent email assistant.**

- Smart prioritization and triage
- Follow-up reminders and tracking
