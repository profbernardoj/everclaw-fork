## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"Work smarter. Not harder."**

OfficeClaw is the AI assistant for the workplace — meeting prep, task consolidation, document search, and communication summaries.

### Value Proposition Statement
> Your workday is meetings, emails, Slack, and documents. Task lists scattered across apps. Important files you can't find. Meetings you forgot to prep for.
> 
> Connect OfficeClaw and get an AI that:
> - Prepares meeting agendas and generates follow-ups
> - Consolidates tasks from Notion, Asana, Todoist, everywhere
> - Finds any document across any platform instantly
> - Summarizes Slack, Teams, and email threads
> - Runs locally — your work data stays private

## X/Twitter / LinkedIn Content Strategy

### Account Setup
- **Handle:** @OfficeClaw
- **Bio:** "AI for work. Meeting prep. Task consolidation. Document search. officeclaw.com"
- **Profile Image:** OfficeClaw logo (professional, clean)
- **Header:** Workplace organization visualization

### Posting Cadence
- **3-4 posts per day**
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM EST (work start), 12-2 PM EST (lunch), 5-7 PM EST (end of day)
- **Focus:** Productivity, meeting efficiency, task management

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Meeting Chaos (30%)
Unprepared meetings, forgotten follow-ups.

#### Pillar 2: Task Fragmentation (30%)
Tasks scattered across apps, consolidation.

#### Pillar 3: Document Chaos (25%)
Finding files, search across platforms.

#### Pillar 4: Privacy (15%)
Work data stays local.

### Sample Posts

**Meeting:**
> Meeting in 5 minutes.
> 
> → no agenda
> → no prep
> → no follow-up
> 
> OfficeClaw: "Here's the agenda. Here's the prep. Here's the follow-up."
> 
> Actually productive meetings.

**Tasks:**
> Tasks in Notion. Tasks in Asana. Tasks in Todoist. Tasks in email.
> 
> One view. Finally.

**Documents:**
> "Where's that document from the Q3 meeting?"
> 
> → search Google Drive
> → search Dropbox

> 
> → OfficeClaw: "Here it is"

**Privacy:**
> Your work documents contain:
> - Strategy plans

> 
> Keep it local.
