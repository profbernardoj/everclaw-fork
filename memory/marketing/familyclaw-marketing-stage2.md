## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"Your family deserves an assistant. Not a data collector."**

FamilyClaw is the AI assistant for families — calendar coordination, birthday reminders, parenting help, and meal planning. All running locally on your hardware.

### Value Proposition Statement
> You're managing a family. School pickups, doctor appointments, birthday parties, meal planning — and that's before your job. It's chaos.
> 
> Connect FamilyClaw and get an AI that:
> - Coordinates everyone's calendars in one place
> - Reminds you of birthdays, appointments, and commitments
> - Helps with parenting questions and activity ideas
> - Plans meals and generates grocery lists
> - Runs locally — your family's data stays in your home

## X/Twitter Content Strategy

### Account Setup
- **Handle:** @FamilyClaw
- **Bio:** "AI for families. Calendar coordination. Birthday reminders. Parenting help. Meal planning. familyclaw.com"
- **Profile Image:** FamilyClaw logo (warm, family-friendly)
- **Header:** Family coordination visualization

### Posting Cadence
- **3-4 posts per day**
- **Best times:** 7-9 AM EST (morning routine), 8-10 PM EST (evening wind-down)
- **Focus:** Relatable family content, parenting tips, organization

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Relatable Chaos (30%)
"What I forgot" moments, family coordination struggles.

#### Pillar 2: Organization Tips (25%)
Calendar coordination, reminder strategies, meal planning.

#### Pillar 3: Privacy & Trust (20%)
"Your kids' data stays home" messaging.

#### Pillar 4: Parenting Help (25%)
Activity ideas, parenting questions, family bonding.
