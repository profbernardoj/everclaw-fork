### If Website Goes Down
- Post: "We're experiencing high traffic. Back soon."
- Redirect to EverClaw.xyz as backup
- Have static landing page ready

### If Negative Replies
- Acknowledge legitimate concerns
- Don't feed trolls
- Focus on ownership and privacy messaging

### If "AI-generated" Skepticism
- Lean into it: "Yes, an AI created this. That's the point."
- Show the timeline: 6 hours, 28 agents, 56 documents
- This proves the multiplier effect
